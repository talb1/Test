About

A quiet eye
for real moments.

Lydia doesn't direct — she observes. Her photographs come from trust built over months, not poses arranged in minutes. She arrives early, stays late, and works in a way that most people forget they're being photographed.

Based in Stuart, Florida. Available for weddings, elopements, and portrait sessions around the world.
