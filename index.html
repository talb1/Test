About Lydia

A quiet presence
in your biggest moments.

Lydia doesn't direct — she observes. Her work is built on trust developed over months of conversation, not poses arranged in minutes. She arrives before the day begins and stays until it ends.

Based in Stuart, Florida. Photographing weddings, elopements, and lifestyle sessions on the Treasure Coast and beyond — wherever the light is worth chasing.
